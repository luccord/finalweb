History

Since the earliest known recordings of history, Möldovastan has played a part in some way. On the sandy shores you can find anything from dinosaur bones (We think they're dinosaur bones) to coinage of ancient dynasties. It's complex, it doesn't make a whole lot of sense, but its woven into the very identity of this great land.

It is our duty, then, to ensure that this great history is perserved. Why wouldn't it? Why shouldn't it? Because of some silly dispute with various geopolitical organizations who play favourites, the story of Möldovastan is at risk of being dulled and forgotten. We created this page to act as a snapshot into what this land whispers to us.
